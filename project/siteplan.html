Site Purpose

The purpose of AdriTravel is to share travel tips, destination guides, budgeting advice, and personal stories. The site aims to inspire and help others plan memorable trips with practical advice and engaging content.

Scenarios

  • Question: What are the best places to visit in Ho Chi Minh City?
    Content Idea: Create a detailed guide with must-see landmarks, local dining options, and cultural spots.
  • Question: How can I pack efficiently for a two-week trip?
    Content Idea: Develop a blog post with packing tips, including a downloadable checklist for travelers.

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: Deep Blue (#2C3E50) - Used for headings, navigation bar, and links.
  • Secondary Color: Emerald Green (#2ECC71) - Used for buttons and accent elements.
  • Background Color: Light Gray (#ECF0F1) - Used for overall page background.

Typography

  • Heading Font: Poppins Bold - Used for page titles and headings.
  • Body Font: Lato Regular - Used for articles and blog content.
  • Special Font: Dancing Script - Used for quotes and personal travel stories.
